# Derrol's Island Tours

This repository holds the website for **Derrol's Island Tours**, a small tour operation on **Roatán, Honduras**.

## Who is Derrol?

**Derrol Jackson** is the captain behind the business. He was born and raised on Roatán, not dropped in from somewhere else for tourist season. He has spent about **fourteen years** working in tourism, mostly on the water, on trails, and in the places that do not always show up in a generic guidebook.

Today he runs **private tours** with his own **25-foot boat** and a crew he trusts. The idea is simple: small groups, real experiences, and no shortcuts.

## What he offers

Every outing is **private** and **flexible**. You work with Derrol and the crew, not a middleman at a desk far from the island.

Guests can book experiences such as:

- **Mangrove tours** through Roatán's calm mangrove channels  
- **Shark snorkeling** in clear, shallow water  
- **Island tours** that mix land and sea, beaches, and spots locals actually know  
- **Local food** and time at **Sandy Bay**  
- **Wildlife and nature** outings (for example the animal park)  
- **ATV rides** on the island's inland trails  

Pricing is handled directly with Derrol when you reach out, so you get a straight answer for your dates and group.

## The website

The live site is **[derrolsislandtours.com](https://derrolsislandtours.com)**. It is written in **English** and **Spanish**, with photos from real tours and a clear path to **book or ask questions on WhatsApp**. No agency wall between you and the person who runs the boat.

If you are visiting Roatán and want the island explained by someone who grew up there, that is what this site is for.
